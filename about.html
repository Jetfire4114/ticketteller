About Us

What is Ticket Teller?

Ticket Teller is an online "ticket blog" of sorts that started back in 2012 finding and listing local ticket prices for events near downtown Orlando Florida. Since then, we've grown from being a small unbacked company to now a full blown corporation that looks at local venues and takes note of all the new shows, plays, or musicals that are, or will be, happening there and relays that information right here for all of you to see. Now, Ticket Teller looks forward to continuing this "trend" of upward groth in the company and gracfully looks forward to your return.

What is our mission?

Here at Ticker Teller Co., our mission is to bring to you any and all up to date events, concerts, shows, musicals, plays, etc. right to your front door, metaphorically, of course (No Ticket Teller employee will arrive at your door at any time). Our overarching goal as a company is the accuracy and reliability of the information we give to you on our website, we make sure of this by having a dedicated team that looks for any erros or inaccurate discriptions of dates and prices of tickets we find. We also make sure that any external or third-party website/company is treated fairly and in good standing, this is because Ticket Teller is not affiliated with any company outside out ready-made list of partners.
